# Better Sleep

Better Sleep is a modern Chrome Extension designed to help you optimize your sleep schedule using sleep cycles. It features a smart sleep planner, bedtime reminders, and insights to help you wake up feeling refreshed.

## Features

- **Sleep Cycles Calculation**: Calculates the best times to go to sleep or wake up based on 90-minute sleep cycles.
- **Sleep Planner**:
  - **Wake Up Mode**: Set your target wake time and see the best times to fall asleep. Includes a "Sleep Now" analysis.
  - **Sleep Now Mode**: See when you should wake up if you go to sleep immediately.
- **Smart Reminders**: Get gentle notifications when it is time to wind down.
- **Sleep Tracking**: Simple one-click tracking to log your sleep duration and mood upon waking.
- **Visual Insights**: View your weekly sleep patterns and get personalized insights about your sleep quality.
- **Multi-language Support**: Available in English, Vietnamese, Japanese, French, Chinese, and Korean.
- **Privacy First**: All data is stored locally on your device.

## Installation

1. Clone or download this repository.
2. Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Enable "Developer mode" in the top right corner.
4. Click "Load unpacked" and select the project folder.

## Usage

1. **Click the extension icon** to open the dashboard.
2. **Set your schedule**: Use the Planner to find your optimal sleep/wake times.
3. **Turn on Reminders**: Enable notifications to stay on track.
4. **Track Sleep**: Click "Going to sleep" when you bed down, and "I'm awake" in the morning.
