Why Better Sleep?

Sleep Insights

Track your sleep duration and mood to understand your patterns over time.

How It Works

01

Plan Your Sleep

Open the Planner tab. Choose "I want to wake at" to find the best bedtime, or "I want to sleep at" to see when to wake up. We handle the math.

02

Set & Forget

Click a suggested time to set your schedule. The extension will remind you when it is time to wind down.

03

Track & Analyze

Click "Going to sleep" when you're ready. In the morning, tell us how you feel to build your personal sleep graph.
